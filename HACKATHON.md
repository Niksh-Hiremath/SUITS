## FAQs (Event Page)

**What if I can't make it last minute?**
We get it, things come up. If you can't make it, just mark "Opt-out" so we can open the spot to someone else. Note: No refunds will be issued for cancellations or no-shows, since spots are limited and reserved in advance.

**Is food provided?**
Yes — meals and refreshments are taken care of. Show up with your laptop, we'll handle the rest.

**Can I bring my team?**
Every attendee must register and be approved individually. If you're attending as a team, each team member must be approved.

**Who can attend?**
Made for senior builders: SDE3s, Product Leads, Founders, and FDEs.

**How long is the event?**
8 hours.

**Where is the Buildathon happening?**
The exact venue will be revealed once your registration has been approved.

**What is Hermes?**
Hermes by Nous Research is the world's fastest-growing open-source AI agent, with 180,000 GitHub stars in under four months. It's the AI agent powering NVIDIA RTX AI PCs, and the platform you'll build with throughout the Buildathon.

**Can I apply if I'm not a GrowthX member?**
Yes. The Buildathon is open to everyone. GrowthX membership is not required.

**Do I get the credits even if I don't win?**
Yes. The participation credits are available to every approved builder. Winners receive additional credits and prizes.

**When do applications close?**
Applications close in 72 hours or earlier if all seats are filled.

**Will I get a refund if my application is rejected?**
Yes. If your application is not accepted, you'll receive a full refund. The registration fee is only meant to ensure high-intent participation and reduce no-shows, and GrowthX does not make any profit from it.
